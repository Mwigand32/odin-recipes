Homemade Pizza

Description

Tangy tomato sauce and fresh cheese spread over a crispy crust! Top it with pepperoni and any other toppings you like. Get creative!

This dish is perfect for a tasty dinner or as an appetizing snack for you and your friends!

Equipment

  • Baking Sheet
  • Parchment Paper

Ingredients

  • 16oz ball of Pizza Dough
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 3 oz sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 5 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2.5 oz pepperoni slices
  • 2 tbsp shredded parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 550 degrees (or highest possible setting for your oven).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on top of a sheet of parchment paper, moving it outward from the center and stretching gently into a circular shape.
  3. Pour pizza sauce onto the pizza dough and evenly distribute it. Place the mozzarella cheese slices and shredded mozzarella over the sauce. Top pizza with pepperoni slices, and any other desired toppings. Sprinkle parmesan cheese over all of the pizza toppings.
  4. Transer topped pizza to the oven and bake for 8-12 minutes, based on your temperature setting on the oven. Check periodically to ensure pizza is cooking evenly.
  5. Let cool for 3-5 minutes and slice evenly.
