Denver Omelette

Description

A hearty breakfast to start the day! Fluffy eggs mixed together with ham, bell peppers, onion, and cheese! Perfect with some strips of bacon and toast!

Equipment

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Egg Beater
  • Medium Skillet
  • Spatula

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup diced ham
  • 2 tbsp diced onion
  • 2 tbsp diced bell peppers
  • 1/3 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • Dash of salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Crack eggs and pour into a mixing bowl. Beat eggs until combined.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Once melted, add ham, onion, and bell pepper to the skillet. Top with salt and pepper and cook for about 5 minutes until caramelized.
  3. Set heat to medium-low and add eggs to the mix. Use a spatula to evenly distribute everything in the skillet. Top the mix with chedder cheese and cayenne pepper.
  4. Continue cooking the omelette in the skillet for about 5 minutes until the omelette is still moist but no longer runny.
  5. Once complete, fold omelette in half and set on a plate to serve.
