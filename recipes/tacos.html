Grilled Chicken Street Tacos

Description

Marinated grilled chicken topped with salsa, onions, and cilantro, all wrapped in a soft tortilla shell! Feel free to make it as spicy or mild as you like!

Equipment

  • Gallon-Size Resealable Bag
  • Outdoor Grill
  • Probe Thermometer

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 24 street taco size tortillas
  • 1/3 cup minced white onion
  • 1/3 cup minced cilantro
  • 4 lime wedges
  • 1 bottle salsa

Directions

  1. Place chicken thighs, lime juice, vegetable oil, minced garlic, cumin, coriander, and chili powder into a gallon-size resealable bag and mix evenly. Marinate the mix for 1-2 hours.
  2. Preheat outdoor grill at high heat and oil surface of the grill.
  3. Remove chicken from the bag after marinating. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes on each side. Use a probe thermometer to ensure the chicken is cooked to 165 degrees Farenheit at the center. Let stand for 5 minutes after cooking, then chop the chicken into small pieces.
  4. Place tortillas on the grill for about 20 to 30 seconds in stacks of two. Flip each stack and heat for an additional 20 to 30 seconds.
  5. Fill each tortilla with chicken, onion, cilantro, and salsa. Serve tacos with a lime wedge.
